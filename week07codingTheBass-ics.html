Why play the bass? There are several reasons to choose to play the bass. The bass is fun to play, it is a diverse instrument, and the bass in an integral part of the sound a band produces. Most instruments are fun to play but the bass has a unique, though sometimes understated, personality. The bass is considered to be part of the rhythm section but can play harmonies and sometimes even melody. This instrument is a team player. A good bassist makes the rest of the band sound amazing.

"A band is essentially a drummer and a bassist plus assorted novelty acts" - Pink Floyd's Nick Mason
