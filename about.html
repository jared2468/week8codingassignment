The Bearded Bassist: Jared Hollingsworth, A.K.A. The Bearded Bassist started playing guitar in the early to mid 1990's. His first love was a Fender Stratocaster that he saved up to buy working at a local fast food restaurant. For the next several years he played off and on but it wasn't until about a year ago when he decided to buy a bass too "mess around with" and ended up liking so much that it quickly become his main instrument of choice. He now jams regularly and plays with a band at the Valley Music Institute.





Influences:

  • Geddy Lee - Rush
  • Les Claypool - Primus, Oysterhead, Duo de Twang and countless other side projects
  • Flea - Red Hot Chili Peppers
  • Mike Watt - THE MINUTEMEN, fIREHOSE
  • John Paul Jones - Led Zeppelin
